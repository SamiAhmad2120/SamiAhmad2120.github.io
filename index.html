About Me:

Hello, my name is Sami, I'm a student at the CUNY College of Staten Island. I am on track to get a bachelors degree in Information Systems and Informatics. I switched to Information Systems from Computer Science because I had learned that I was more keen on the application theory of computers rather than the language of computer development. I love learning about computers and how they work, I build computers in my spare time whenever I'm asked to help friends/family. I hope to pursue more of computers other than just what I'm learning in class.

I am a second generation Arab American, I'm half Palestinian and half Syrian. I am also a Muslim which is the core of who I am. I have always been fascinated by religion as a whole and have self-studied almost every major religion and their respective history. Ever since I was a little kid I've been fascinated by history. I love reading and studying the many different eras and events of humanity, examples being the Roman Empire, the discovery of America, and the Celtic takeover of Europe in the 1200's. I used to be a competitive swimmer in high school, and swimming is very much still a part of my life. I am a swim instructor and a swim coach, I coach for the Staten Island Aquatics Club (SIAC) and also run private lessons at the YMCA. I love working in teams and have always worked best in groups. I enjoy public speaking as it allows my voice to be heard, and I hope that any chance I get I can further prove my dedication into anything I set my mind to.
